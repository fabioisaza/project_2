Counties by Winning Party
Using the Treemap above we found multiple patterns that clearly displayed changing trends in voting patterns and differences in County behaviors over the previous 4 Presidential election. At first sight, one can visibly deduce that big Counties such as Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach tend to vote Democratic, while smaller counties with less volume in population each tend to vote Republican. Despite the population of each county, more Republican votes for every election have been casted. And in 2016, the overall turnout for republicans was much larger than any of the previous years.
